Global trust header (top of page)

Add a single trust line directly under the collection title and intro sentence.

Format: star icons + average rating + total review count.

Example copy: "★★★★☆ 4.7 average rating from 1,200+ happy homes"

This is global across the collection, not per product.

Section structure and “popular” highlight without badges

For each section on the collection page:

Place 1 standout product as the first item in that section.

Add slightly more top padding or spacing above the first product card only (subtle emphasis).

Keep the rest of the product grid identical (no new tags, ribbons, or badges).

Add one helper line of text once per section, above the grid, not on product cards.

Example: "Most loved picks for small dorm rooms"

Keep it short, friendly, and specific to the section.

Social proof microcopy under price (muted text)

Under the price on product cards, add one short line of muted social proof text.

Do not add icons or badges.

Examples:

"500+ saved on Pinterest"

"Seen in 1,000+ cozy spaces"

"A community favorite"

If we do not have real counts, use ranges or soft phrasing (ex: "Hundreds saved on Pinterest") and keep it consistent.

Testimonials strip (horizontal)

Add a horizontal testimonial strip near the top of each collection page (below the global trust header, above the first section).

3 to 6 short testimonials.

Each testimonial includes: star rating + 1 sentence + first name + optional date.

Keep it lightweight and scrollable on mobile, no modal, no long review blocks.

Constraints / guardrails

No badges, ribbons, “best seller” tags, discount stickers, or extra labels on product cards.

Do not push products below the fold with large UI blocks.

Maintain the current grid layout and spacing style.

Acceptance criteria

All collection pages show the global trust header.

Each section shows one helper line and a subtly emphasized first product via spacing only.

Each product card shows one muted social proof line under the price.

Testimonials strip is visible and horizontally scrollable on mobile.
